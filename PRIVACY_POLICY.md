# Privacy Policy for HabitFlow

**Last updated:** January 2, 2026

HabitFlow ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data in our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect any personal data.** 

HabitFlow is an offline-first application. All data regarding your habits, progress, statistics, and settings is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to any external servers, cloud storage, or third-party analytics services.

## 2. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions to function correctly:
*   **Vibration/Haptics:** To provide tactile feedback when you interact with the app.
*   **Storage (Optional):** Only if you choose to export or share an image of your progress, the app may temporarily write to your device's storage.

## 3. Data Safety

Since all data is stored on your device, you have full control over it.
*   **Retention:** Your data remains on your device until you uninstall the application or clear the app data in your settings.
*   **Deletion:** Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data associated with it.

## 4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we need not take action as we do not collect data. As an offline app, parents have full control over the device usage.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
